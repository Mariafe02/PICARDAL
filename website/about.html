About

Cainta Senior High School (CAISEN) is a public secondary school located in Cainta, Rizal, Philippines. It was founded in 2016. We offer quality education to Students in Grade 11 and 12, with focus on academic exellence and personal growth. The school has a well-equipped library, computer laboratories, and modern classrooms that cater to the needs of its students. We have a team of highly quialified and dedicated teachers who are passionate about teaching and mentoring your minds. Cainta Senior High School has a diverse student population, with students coming from different backgrounds and cultures. The school fosters a culture of respect, tolerance, and inclusivity, ensuring that every student feels valued and supported. Caints Senior High school is committed to the continuous improvement of its educational programs and facilities. The school is always looking for ways to enhance the learning experience of its students and to provide them with the best possible education.
